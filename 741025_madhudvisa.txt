Melbourne

My dear Madhudvisa Swami:

Please accept my blessings. In India ghee is required for our temples. I want to now if you can supply 200 lbs. per month in exchange for books.

I hope this meets you in good health.

Your ever well wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami
