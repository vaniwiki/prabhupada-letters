Calcutta

Dear Rajkumar,

Please accept my blessings. I am in due receipt of your letter dated 16 September and have noted the contents. You may please come and see me here in Vrndavana. I will be here until October 8th.

I hope this meets you in good health.

Your ever well-wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami
