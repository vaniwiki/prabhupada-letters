====== Dr. Wolf ======

<card class="letter">
My dear Dr. Wolf,

Please accept my blessings. I am in due receipt of your letter dated 1st of December, '76.

I'm glad to hear that you are coming to India in the near future. Yes, Krsna wants you, don't try to avoid Him. You are a sincere devotee.

Hoping this will meet you in good health.

Your ever well-wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami

ACBS/jda
</card>
