====== Punjab National Bank ======

<card class="letter">
Resolution

At the meeting of the members of the Bureau of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness held at their Head Office at the Hare Krishna Land, Juhu, Bombay on the 19th November, 1974, the following resolved:

1. That the Savings Account No. 1123 be enclosed and that the current balance be deposited in the Current Account to be opened.

Signed:

A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami

Brahmananda Swami

Datted this 19th Day of November, 1974
