Manager, Trade Bank & Trust Co.\\
2nd Avenue at 4th Street\\
New York, N.Y. 10003

Dear Sir,

Please transfer by __air mail__ three hundred and eighty dollars and no cents ($380.00), to the Bank of Baroda LTD., Chandni Chowk, Delhi, India, for credit of my savings account No. 1432 And debit my savings account No. B 19282 with you.

My passbook is enclosed herewith, which please make up and return to me along with your mail transfer receipt, and oblige.

Thanking you in anticipation.

Yours sincerely,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami

By registered post with A.D.\\
Enclosure-Passbook
