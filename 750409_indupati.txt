Indupati Brahmacari\\
Sri Caitanya Gaudiya Math\\
Mathura Rd.\\
Vrndavana---281 121

My dear Sripada Brahmacariji,

Please accept my humble obeisances. I am in due receipt of your letter (in Bengali) dated 29-3-75. I am advising my men in Vrndavana to begin the function from the 16th of April as desired by you. Thanking you for your suggestion.

Yours respectfully,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami
