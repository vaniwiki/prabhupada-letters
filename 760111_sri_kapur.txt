International Foundation of Meditation\\
44 Kailash Mandar,\\
Kanpur

Dear Sri Kapur,

Please accept my best wishes. I beg to thank you for your letter dated December 25th, 1975. I shall be returning to Vrindaban by the end of March. At that time I shall try to visit Kanpur to see the park.

Hoping this meets you well.

Yours sincerely,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami
