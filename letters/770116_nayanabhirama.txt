====== Letter to: Nayanabhirama ======

New York

Dear Nayanabhirama dasa,

Please accept my blessings. I am in due receipt of your letter dated January 4th. Yes, you are welcome to come to India if you like, to work in Bombay.

Hoping this will meet you in good health.

Your ever well-wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami

ACBS/rds
