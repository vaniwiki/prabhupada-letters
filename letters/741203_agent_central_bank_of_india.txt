Re: Credit of interest on L.T.D. 5/17 for Rs. 73,000/- to my H/S A/c 831 with you.

Dear Sir:

I am in due receipt of your letter dated November 21, 1974 and have noted the contents. As requested by you I am herewith enclosing herewith the Deposit Receipt as referred to above duly endorsed by me for crediting the interest monthly to my S/A/c 821. As I am now out of Calcutta, the Deposit Receipt certificate may be kept in your custody until I collect it upon my return.\\
I am also enclosing herewith one check for Dollar 1000.00 No. %%_____%% which please credit to my S A/c 831 and oblige.

Please acknowledge receipt.

Thanking you.

Faithfully yours,\\
