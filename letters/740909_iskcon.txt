====== Resolution ======

RESOLUTION

At the meeting of the members of the Bureau of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness held at their Head Office at the Hare Krishna Land, Gandhi Gram Road, Juhu, Bombay on the 9th September, 1974, the following was resolved:

1. That the name of Sriman Mansvi das Adhikary, a signatory in the current bank account held with Central Bank of India, Gowalia Tank Road Branch, Bombay may be deleted.

2. That the name of Sriman Bhugarba das Adhikary may be added to the above account, whose signature appears below.

3. That the account will be operated by Sriman Giriraj das Brahmachary and any one of the two other signatories, namely Sriman Yasomatinandan das Adhikary and Sriman Bhugarba das Adhikary.

Attest

A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami\\
\\
Gurudas Adhikary
