====== Letter to: Kaumadaki ======

Los Angeles

My dear Kaumadaki devi dasi,

Please accept my blessings. This is to acknowledge reciept of your Sri Vyasa Puja offering, August 30, 1975. Thank you very much for it.

Your ever well wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami
