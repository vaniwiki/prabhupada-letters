====== Dhananjaya ======

Vrndavana

My dear Dhananjaya das;

Please accept my blessings. Kindly send me a report immediately to the Johannesburg address.

Hoping this meets you in good health.

Your ever well wisher;\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami
