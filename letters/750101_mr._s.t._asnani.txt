====== Mr. S.T. Asnani ======

S.T. Asnani, MA\\
Advocate High Court\\
"Meher House"\\
15, Cawasji Patel St.\\
Fort, Bombay-1

My dear Mr. Asnani,

Please accept my greetings. I thank you very much for your kind New Year's card. The picture of Krishna on the front was very nice.

Thanking you once again.

Your ever well-wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami
