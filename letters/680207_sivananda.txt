====== Letter to: Sivananda ======

My dear Sivananda,

Please accept my blessings. I hope you are continuing to study Bengali. I shall be glad to know how much progress you have made by this time. Hope you are well.

Your ever well wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami
