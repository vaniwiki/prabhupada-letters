====== Kirtanananda ======

<card class="letter">
New Vrindaban

My dear Kirtanananda Swami:

Please accept my blessings. I am in due receipt of your telegram. Upon your recommendation I am accepting the following as my initiated disciples and their spiritual names are as follows: Barbara Wolfe---Vedanta dasi; Janet Cleaver---Viduttama dasi; Kurt Cleaver---Sarvasaksi dasa; Florence Walldeck---Vedapriya dasi; Carlos Ordenoz---Nityodita dasa; Gary Williams---Rsi Kumar dasa; Jeff Long---MahaRudra dasa; Jeff Wiseman---Atmabhu dasa; Jeff Daniels---Visvamohan dasa; Eileen Daniels---Vinalangi dasi; Karen Bogdonoff---Janani dasi.

I also accept for second initiated brahmanas the following: Suksmarupini dasi, Damagraha dasi, Dharmakala dasi, Maha Kamini dasi, Nogomala dasi, Kunjari dasi, Jalakolahali dasa, Baivsyhe dasa, Caidayastru das, Hari Dharma dasa, Advaitacarya dasa. Enclosed are the sanctified threads and mantra sheet.

I hope this meets you in good health.

Your ever well wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami

ACBS/bs
</card>
