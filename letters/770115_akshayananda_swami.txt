====== Letter to: Akshayananda Swami ======

Krsna Balarama Mandir\\
Raman Reti,\\
Vrindavana, Mathura, U.P.

KUMBHA MELA, ALLAHABAD

My dear Akshayananda Maharaj,

Please accept my blessings. This is to introduce Sri Bajrang Bali Brahmacari. Please give him a place in our ashram in Sri Vrindavana Dham, where he may remain until my arrival.

Your ever well-wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami

A.C.B.S./hsd
