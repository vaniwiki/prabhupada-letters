====== Letter to: Krsna Devi ======

My dear Krishna devi,

Please accept my blessings. You will read the letter of your husband & try to know my mind. I am coming very soon to see you when everything will be adjusted. Hope you are well.

Yours sincerely,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami
