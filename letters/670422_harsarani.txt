====== Harsarani ======

<card class="letter">
My dear Srimati Harsarani,

Please accept my blessings. I have duly received your bags for the beads and thank you very much. I hope you are %%__%% Kirtana nicely and feeling transcendental happiness. Please offer my blessings to all the boys and girls. Hope to hear from you.

Your ever well-wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami
