====== Puranjana, Bhaja Hari, Jananananda, Rohininandana, Partha, Mahavisnu, Nara Haridasa, Patita Uddharanadas, Mukundadas ======

Bhaktivedanta Manor

My Dear Puranjana, Bhaja Hari,

Jananananda, Rohininandan, Partha das,\\
Maha Visnu, Nara Haridasa, Patita\\
Uddharanadas, and Mukundadas:

Please accept my blessings. I am in due receipt of your letter dated October 14, 1974 hand delivered by Puranjana das: Regarding the intended election, I have no preference for anyone. Whomever gets the majority election, he has my vote.

I hope this meets you all well.

Your ever well wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami
