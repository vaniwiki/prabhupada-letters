My dear Mokika dasa, Kardama Muni dasa, and Vaisnavananda dasa,

Please accept my blessings. I am in due receipt of your letter dated August 6 and enclosed daksina and I thank you very much.

I hope this meets you all in good health.

Your ever well-wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami
