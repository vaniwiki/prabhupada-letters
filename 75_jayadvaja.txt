My dear Jayadvaja das,

Please accept my blessings. Your name is not Jaya Dharma but Jayadvaja das. Please chant 16 rounds and follow the 4 regulative principles.

I hope this meets you in good health.

Your ever well-wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami
