====== Letter to: Gopiparanadhana ======

My dear Gopiparanadhana Prabhu,

Please accept my blessings. I beg to acknowledge receipt of your letter dated November 20, 1975. Yes! You have done it very nicely. I thank you very much, so immediately it can be printed in BTG and then later on it can be published in book form. I thank you very much for your sincere efforts, please continue to work sincerely and Krishna will bless you more and more.

I hope this letter meets you well.

Your ever well wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami

ACBS/hda\\
Carbon copy: Satsvarupa Goswami
