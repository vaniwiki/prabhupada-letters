Gandhi Gram Road, Juhu, Bombay 54

RESOLUTION

At the meeting of the members of the Bureau of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness held at their Head Office at the Hare Krishna Land, Gandhi Gram Road, Juhu, Bombay on the 29 July, 1974 the following was resolved:

1. That the name of Sriman Karandhar das Adhikary, a signatory in the current bank account held with the Central Bank of India,Gowalin Tank Road Branch, Bombay may be deleted.

2. That out of the remaining three signatories: namely, Giriraj das Brahmachary, Manesvi das Adhikary, and Yasomatinandan das Adhikary, any two will operate the bank account.
