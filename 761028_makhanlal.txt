St. Louis

My dear Makhanlal,

Please accept my blessings. I am in receipt of your letter dated 13/10/76, along with a check for $25.

Yes, you must rectify this mistake. All society stationary must bear my name as founder-acarya. Please do it immediately.

Your ever well-wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami

ACBS/jda
