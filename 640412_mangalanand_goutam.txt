====== Letter to: Mangalanand Goutam ======

Sri Mangalanand Goutam\\
Jama Musjid Dispensary\\
Delhi-6

My dear Goutamji,

The other day when you came to me with your wife, you said that the order book & receipt book would be returned by you. But since then you have not seen me.

Kindly return back the order book & the receipt book for bearer & oblige.

Yours sincerely,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami
