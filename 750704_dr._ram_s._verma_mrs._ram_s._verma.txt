Dr. & Mrs. Ram S. Verma\\
1325 Madison St. Apt. 203\\
Denver, Colorado 80206

Dear Dr. Verma:

Please accept my blessings. I beg to acknowledge with thanks your letter undated with enclosed donation of $50.00 and thank you very much. You have a good position by the grace of Krishna, and I request you to continue on in this spirit of service to the Lord.

I hope this meets you in good health.

Your ever well wisher,\\
A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami
